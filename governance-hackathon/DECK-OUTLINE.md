# GovernanceFlow — Deck Outline

## Slide 1 — Title
**GovernanceFlow**  
AI-assisted Maintenance Governance Platform

### Isi:
- Nama project
- Nama tim
- Tagline: *Turning maintenance logs into reliable governance data*

---

## Slide 2 — Problem
**The Hidden Governance Problem in Maintenance Operations**

### Poin utama:
- Maintenance data masih dicatat manual
- Format dan penamaan tidak konsisten
- Typo dan duplikasi sering terjadi
- Approval flow lambat
- Sulit dilacak saat data sudah diubah

### Inti pesan:
Masalah utamanya bukan cuma maintenance, tapi kualitas data dan governance-nya.

---

## Slide 3 — Why It Matters
**Bad Records Lead to Bad Decisions**

### Dampak bisnis:
- Review manual membebani supervisor
- Data tidak siap dipakai untuk planning
- Risiko aset sulit diprioritaskan
- Akuntabilitas lemah
- Potensi downtime meningkat

### Inti pesan:
Jika data maintenance tidak bisa dipercaya, keputusan maintenance juga ikut melemah.

---

## Slide 4 — Solution
**GovernanceFlow: Simple, Structured, Accountable**

### Solusi utama:
- Structured submission form
- AI-assisted validation
- Priority review queue
- Governance KPI dashboard
- Audit trail

### Inti pesan:
GovernanceFlow membuat pencatatan maintenance lebih terstandar, tervalidasi, dan mudah diaudit.

---

## Slide 5 — How It Works
**Workflow**

### Step-by-step:
1. Admin submits maintenance record
2. System validates format and data consistency
3. Clean records move forward
4. Suspicious records go to review queue
5. All actions are recorded in audit trail

### Inti pesan:
Sistemnya sederhana untuk user, tapi kuat untuk governance.
