## Project Name

## Category

## Short Description
OPS GUARD is an AI-assisted maintenance operations web app that standardizes maintenance reporting, validates data quality, routes suspicious records for review, and provides a clear audit trail for accountable operational decision-making.

## Full Description
OPS GUARD is built to solve a practical operations challenge in maintenance operations: unreliable records caused by manual, inconsistent, and fragmented data entry.

In many organizations, maintenance records are still created by different teams using different naming conventions, formats, and levels of detail. This leads to typo-prone submissions, duplicate entries, weak validation, delayed approval flow, and poor auditability. When the underlying data is inconsistent, decision-making becomes slower, less accurate, and less accountable.

OPS GUARD addresses this problem through a simple but structured web experience. It introduces a standardized maintenance submission form, AI-assisted validation to detect inconsistencies and anomalies, a priority review queue for supervisors, and a visible audit trail for every important action.

The platform is designed not only to improve operational efficiency, but also to strengthen governance by making maintenance data more trustworthy, reviewable, and decision-ready.

## Problem Statement
Maintenance operations often rely on manually entered records that are inconsistent, difficult to verify, and hard to audit. This creates governance gaps that affect data integrity, accountability, and maintenance planning quality.

## Solution Statement
OPS GUARD standardizes maintenance reporting, performs AI-assisted validation, highlights risky or suspicious records, routes them through a review process, and stores clear audit visibility to support faster and more accountable decisions.
